# Manifold tuning campaign — superseded

The pre-NVMe tuning campaign was derived from the withdrawn Phase-0 stores,
checkpoints, winner pointer, and geometry readouts. Its numerical findings,
candidate identities, gates, and recommendations are therefore unsupported
and have been removed.

The replacement is Phase 0.5. See [paper_comparison.md](paper_comparison.md)
for the staged scientific matrix and [audit_2026-07-20.md](audit_2026-07-20.md)
for the remediation ledger. New geometry evidence must come from the corrected
trained-endpoint pipeline and a Phase-0.5 run binding; it may not inherit a
block identity or conclusion from the superseded campaign.
